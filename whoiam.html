Who I Am

My name is Renee. I do a lot of things. Here is a brief, but kind of detailed account of my life:

  • I have a bachelor's degree in Psychology, a year of graphic design school, and am a Certified Poliquin Personal Trainer.
  • I worked in Sales and Management at AT&T for 10 years.
  • I create art with a variety of mediums including watercolor, acrylic, ink, and charcoal.
  • I take pictures of people.

You can find me at Creator's Space in St.Paul, MN or at Los Campeones Gym in Minneapolis,MN.
